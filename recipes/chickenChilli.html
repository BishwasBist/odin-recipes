Chicken Chilli

Asian Dish Chicken Chilli

The dish that every asian knows and waters down anyone who sees. The famous asian dish chilli chicken simple to make but something you cant get enough of.

Here is the recipe for it

Ingredients

Though the recipe is simple but you'd have to add few spices and asian sauce to your arsenal from asian grocery store.

    Meat: Preferably chicken breast.

    Onion, Capsicum: About half onion and capsicum per head.

    Ketchup and soysauce:I know I know who adds ketchup when cooking, trust me on this one uncle roger.

    Corn FlourYou will need it for two purpose one to fry the chicken pre hand and another to thicken the sauce.

    Spices and seasonings:Luckily this doesn't require much spices due to ketchup. All you need is bit of pepper, chilli powder, salt and sugar(optional).

    Ginger and garlic paste: Now the holy grail of all asian cooking. You will also be needing it for two purpose one to marinate the chicken and another to add at the first thing of cooking

    Egg: Although not compulsory but you can add egg to batter for frying.

Steps:

Here's a very brief overview what to do:

  • Marinate the chicken in ginger garlic paste

  • Add corn flour and egg and water accordingly to make batter

  • Fry the chicken

  • now in another pan add oil in low heat and add one teaspoon of ginergarlic paste

  • Mean while in a cup add bit of corn flour, sauces, and water

  • add the big chucks of onion and capsicum in pan and crank up the heat to high and be quick with it

  • now mix the custom sauce you made as the corn flour tends to settle in bottom and add it to pan

  • add spices and stir until thickend

  • Turn off the heat and VIOLA~
